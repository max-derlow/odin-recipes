Lasagna Recipe

Description

Garfield's favourite.

This is a thicc lasagna. It is good to look at, and even better to eat.

Ingredients

  • 400g pasta
  • 400g pecorino sauce
  • 400g minced meat
  • 200g tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon of Love

Steps

  1. butter the form
  2. put pasta in the form
  3. put minced meat in the form
  4. put tomatoes and sauce into the form
  5. blend
  6. mix in some love
  7. boil for 90 minutes
  8. dispose of it
